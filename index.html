Diamond Sky

Earth observation, satellites, data & emerging technology

Diamond Sky is an independent consultancy working across Earth observation, satellite systems, data infrastructure, and emerging technologies.

We support public agencies, research organisations, and industry partners with strategy, technical analysis, architecture, and communication — particularly where space-based data meets real-world decision-making.

The practice builds on deep experience across international space agencies, Earth observation programmes, and data initiatives — including work with NASA, ESA, JAXA, Geoscience Australia, and CSIRO — alongside many others across the globe, with a focus on clarity, robustness, and long-term value.

Diamond Sky is led by George Dyke, an independent consultant with experience across Earth observation, satellite programmes, and international space and data initiatives.
